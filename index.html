Labels by Use

In todays fast-pace market, branding is one of the main factor that sells a product. For that labels play a vital role. You wont see any product in market without a label on it. LEA focus on ensuring that you get the right exposure for your product by choosing the right label. Our main target audiences can range from small product manufacturing companies to Large volume product manufacturing industries such as Medical Drug industries, Food Product industries, Consumer goods industries, Factory products etc. In short, every manufacturing industry needs labelling to show their product specifications and brand value whether those products are for home use or for industrial use. Below are some of the main uses of labels:

Nutritional Labels

Nutritional labels contain product information designed to allow the consumer to make informed choices about their diet. The nutritional label allows them to learn the nutritional value of the product quickly and efficiently, and to help avoid products that might aggravate existing conditions like high cholesterol or allergies.

Bar-code Labels

The black and white stickers on the bottom or sides of products are often ignored by customers. However, a product's bar-code is crucial. The distributing company must ensure that it adheres to federal standards for bar-code labels, especially when it comes to certain food products or drugs from the pharmaceutical industry.

Beer Labels

The craft brewing industry has exploded in recent years, with estimates indicating it now represents a twenty three billion dollar industry. Therefore it's very crucial for craft brewers looking to break into the market to set themselves apart.Eye-catching labels can be a great way to engage customers and persuade them to choose your brew.

Pharmaceutical Labels

If you aim to deliver products that provide health benefits to consumers, with quality label materials, value-added label solutions and clear label design, it will help you communicate your products benefits and gain your customers trust with pharmaceutical and medical labels.

Cosmetic Labels

Proper product labeling is an important aspect of marketing a cosmetic or personal care product. Labeling is used to help inform consumers of a product's intended use and any related warnings, its ingredients and net quantity of contents, and its place of manufacture or distribution.

Name Badges

Not only can name badges help employees enter and exit a building, they can be efficient and perfect tool for enhanced safety. That way, security guards know whether an individual works in the building or not. These tags act as an ID card for many occasions like conferences, trade shows etc.

Labels by Type

Paper Labels

This is the most common type of blank label material. Paper labels are highly affordable and you can print just about anything on them. Paper labels are also compatible with almost all types of label printers. Paper-based blank labels are the recommended format for home users. Paper labels are cheap and degrade easily.

Synthetic Labels

Blank labels made from synthetic material are highly popular among those who want long-lasting labels. If you want waterproof, heat resistant, or abrasion-free labels, synthetic is an excellent material to choose. Synthetic labels are suitable for both home and industrial use.

High Gloss Labels

If you want colorful and shiny labels, you will require high gloss printing material. High gloss blank labels are perfect for printing labels with pictures or photographs. This material vividly shows of all color and shines under bright lights.

Matte Labels

Matte blank labels are a lot like high gloss ones, except that these don’t shine. If you don’t want a shiny screen on the label, choose matte. This material can also vividly portray colors, but without brightly reflecting light.

Chemical Labels

Product descriptions are important for any company, but when chemical labels are involved, it is especially crucial for businesses to adhere to federal and local law requirements.
